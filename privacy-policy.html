Privacy Policy

SmoothSailor — Last updated: February 19, 2026

Overview

SmoothSailor ("the App") is a mobile application that helps recreational boaters plan safer, more comfortable trips by scoring route conditions. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you choose to provide (display name, experience level, home region). Authentication is handled by Supabase.

Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location to center the map and tag community reports at your current position. Location data is used only while the App is in the foreground and only when you grant permission. We do not track your location in the background.

Route Data

When you draw a route and request scoring, the route coordinates are sent to our backend server to fetch weather, wave, tide, and wind data from NOAA. If you choose to save a route, it is stored in our database associated with your account.

Boat Profiles

Boat specifications you enter (length, beam, draft, comfort preferences) are stored with your account to personalize scoring.

Community Reports

If you submit a community report (ride quality, hazard, etc.), it is stored with your user ID, location, and report type, and is visible to other users nearby.

Scoring Feedback

If you submit feedback on a score, we store your rating and optional comments to improve our scoring accuracy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide route scoring based on real-time environmental data
  • To save and retrieve your routes, boats, and preferences
  • To display community reports to nearby users
  • To improve scoring accuracy based on user feedback

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • NOAA (NWS, NDBC, CO-OPS) — weather, wave, and tide data (route coordinates are sent to NOAA APIs to retrieve environmental conditions)
  • Google Maps / Apple Maps — map display
  • Render — backend API hosting
  • Expo / EAS — app build and update delivery

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. Route coordinates are sent to NOAA APIs solely to retrieve environmental data for scoring.

Data Retention

Account data, saved routes, and boat profiles are retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and associated data by contacting us. Community reports may expire automatically based on their type.

Security

All communication between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device. We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can deny location permissions and still use the App (you'll need to manually navigate the map)
  • You can delete saved routes and boat profiles from within the App
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
