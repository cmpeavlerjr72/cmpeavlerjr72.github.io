Common Questions

Why does scoring take a while?

SmoothSailor fetches real-time data from multiple NOAA sources (weather, waves, tides) for each point along your route. This typically takes 10-20 seconds depending on route length.

Why do I need to grant location access?

Location is used to center the map near you and to tag community reports at your position. You can deny the permission and manually navigate the map instead.

How do scores work?

Each point on your route is scored 0-100 based on wind, waves, wave period, tidal flow, and precipitation relative to your boat's size and comfort settings. Green (great), yellow (ok), orange (rough), red (avoid).

What is the Water Type toggle?

On lakes, tidal data doesn't apply. Set it to "Lake" to skip tide lookups for faster, more accurate scoring. "Auto" detects this automatically based on your route's distance from the nearest tidal station.
